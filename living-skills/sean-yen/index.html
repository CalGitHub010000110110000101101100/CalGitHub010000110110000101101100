Other name things

My name means nothing to me, and it holds no cultural significance.

My parents named me after a character in The Sixth Sense; I haven’t watched the movie yet though. They realized my name was hard for native Chinese speakers to pronounce, so they chose a more suited one for my brother.

I like my name because it’s short and monosyllabic, but perhaps it could be shorter and more intuitive to spell, but then it’ll look too Chinese and affect some people’s initial perceptions before meeting me. I prefer Irish spelling because it’s shorter.
